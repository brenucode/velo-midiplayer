Click a slot, then press any key — a letter, symbol, or a mouse side-button (M4/M5). Press Delete to unbind it (None), or Esc to cancel. Hotkeys work even when Velo is in the background.
Sound — "playing" illusion
Plays the note (real piano sample) or a real mechanical key sound in the app, synced to the song. Extra keyboard models stream online (cached after first use); the default brown works offline. To make it come out of your microphone (so others hear you "playing"): install a virtual audio cable (VB-CABLE), route Velo's audio to it in Windows sound settings, and pick it as your mic in Discord.
Humanize — natural feel
Roll43
Timing40

Velocity52
Pick a profile as a starting point, then fine-tune the sliders — your settings are saved and restored next time. Roll = how much chords spread (and re-randomise every chord) · Timing = looseness of each note · Rubato = how much the tempo breathes · Velocity = force variation. Each profile keeps re-randomising so it never repeats. Off = exact mechanical original.
Random Fail — play like a human who slips
Timing slips5
Wrong notes5
Adds occasional human mistakes to playback — the opposite of Humanize. Timing slips = how often a note rushes or drags · Wrong notes = how often a note slips to the wrong key (up or down, by up to an octave). Off = perfect, exact playback. Heads up: this intentionally plays notes that aren't in the file, so keep the sliders low for a subtle, believable effect.
